Welcome to dice game!

We just have a few rules

  • Rule 1: You can roll as much as you want. If you click bank, your combined rolled score will go to your bank.
  • Rule 2: There is always a catch. If you roll 1, you will lose whatever you collected but didnt put in bank and then its next player turn!
  • Rule 3: If you roll 6, well you lose something. You lose whatever you got from your current state and you lose 6 from the bank. And it's not your turn anymore.
  • Goal of the game: Reach over 51 point by rolling the dice.
  • First set up names for players. Than press start to, well start obivusly.
